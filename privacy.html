Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Summary. Next Visit is a private space for two people. Your photos, memories, and lists are visible only to you and the one partner you invite — never public, never sold. We do not run advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking. We collect the minimum we need to operate the Service, and you can delete your account and personal information at any time. This summary is for convenience only; the numbered terms below govern.

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how Next Visit ("Next Visit", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information in connection with the Next Visit application at next-visit.app and the trip planner at go.next-visit.app (together, the "Service"). Next Visit is operated from Queensland, Australia. By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

1. Scope of this Policy

  1. This Policy applies to the personal information we process about users of the Service.
  2. The Service is intended for individuals aged 18 years or older. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children (see clause 11).
  3. This Policy does not apply to third-party products, websites, or services that we do not own or control, including the travel providers you reach through links in the planner. Your use of those services is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

2. Information we collect

  1. Information you provide. Depending on how you use the Service, you may provide:
    1. your first name, which you enter when you begin using the app;
    2. an optional date of birth, if you choose to add it to your profile;
    3. an optional email address, only if you choose to link one for account recovery (we use a one-time sign-in link or a six-digit code; there are no passwords);
    4. pairing details when you connect with a partner, including the names you each choose, a private pairing code that links your two devices into one shared space, and details you add such as your anniversary date; and
    5. content you create, including the trips you save (destinations, dates, and place names), photos and videos you add together with their captions, your chosen song, shared lists (such as a bucket list and dream destinations), quiz answers, and notes.
  2. Information collected automatically. When you use the Service, our hosting and infrastructure providers automatically record limited technical information, such as your Internet Protocol (IP) address, device and browser characteristics, operating system, and date and time stamps. This information is used to operate and secure the Service, to troubleshoot, and to prevent abuse (for example, rate-limiting requests). If you choose to enable notifications, we also store a push notification token so that we can send the alerts you have enabled.
  3. Media metadata. Photos and videos you add are stored as you provide them. If a file you upload contains embedded information (such as the date taken, or location data recorded by your camera), that information forms part of the file you choose to add.
  4. Information we do not collect. For clarity, the Service does not:
    1. use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies, and does not use advertising identifiers;
    2. request or collect precise location from your device's operating system (the app does not ask for location permission);
    3. access your contacts or microphone, or scan your photo library (adding media uses your device's standard file picker);
    4. collect payment or financial information — this version of the Service has no in-app purchases or subscriptions and sells nothing; or
    5. collect health or fitness data.

3. How we use your information

We process personal information for the following purposes:

  1. to create and operate your account and your private shared space, and to keep it in working order;
  2. to pair your devices with your partner and synchronise your shared content between you in real time;
  3. to send a sign-in link or code when you request to recover access, and to send notifications you have enabled;
  4. to provide the artificial-intelligence suggestions you request (see clause 7);
  5. to respond to your enquiries and provide customer support;
  6. to maintain the security and integrity of the Service, troubleshoot problems, and prevent fraud or abuse; and
  7. to comply with applicable legal obligations and to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

4. Legal bases for processing (EEA and UK users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases:

  1. performance of a contract, to provide the Service you have requested;
  2. your consent, which you may withdraw at any time, for processing such as sending you notifications;
  3. our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving the Service, where those interests are not overridden by your rights; and
  4. compliance with a legal obligation to which we are subject.

5. How and with whom we share information

  1. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioural or targeted advertising.
  2. Your partner. The Service is built for two people. Content you add to your couple space — memories, photos, lists, and profile details such as your name and date of birth — is visible to, and editable by, the one partner you have invited, and only to them. There are no public areas of the Service. If you share a trip link generated by the planner, the trip details encoded in that link are visible to anyone you send it to.
  3. Service providers. We share personal information with a limited number of providers who process it on our behalf, under contract, and only as needed to operate the Service:
    1. Google (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Storage, and Firebase Cloud Messaging) — to host your account, database, and media, and to deliver notifications;
    2. Anthropic — to power the AI features described in clause 7, processing the inputs you submit to those features;
    3. Resend — to send account-recovery email;
    4. Vercel — to host the website and our server functions, including standard server logs;
    5. Spotify — to search for songs when you set a song; we send only the search text and do not connect to or read your Spotify account;
    6. Pexels and Wikimedia — to retrieve stock destination imagery by place name; we do not send them your personal information; and
    7. Google Maps Platform (including the Maps and Places APIs) — to convert the city and place names you type into coordinates and to display maps of your trips. We do not access your device's GPS location.
  4. Travel partners (planner). The planner links out to third-party travel services, including Skyscanner, Booking.com, Stay22, Viator, GetYourGuide, Klook, 12Go, Trainline, Rome2Rio, Airalo, SafetyWing, and Flytographer. Some links are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. When you open one of these links, your interaction is with that third party under its own terms and privacy policy.
  5. Legal and safety. We may disclose information where we believe in good faith that it is required by law, or necessary to protect the rights, property, safety, or security of our users, the public, or Next Visit.
  6. Business transfers. We may disclose or transfer information in connection with, or during negotiations of, a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business. We will require the recipient to honour this Policy.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

  1. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.
  2. We store a small number of items on your device using browser storage — such as your light or dark appearance preference and your sign-in state — because they are essential to making the Service work. They are not used to track you across other websites.
  3. Certain third-party booking panels in the planner (for example, Stay22 and GetYourGuide) may set their own cookies when you interact with them, for instance to attribute a booking. Those cookies are set and governed by each partner's own privacy policy, not ours.

7. Artificial-intelligence features

  1. Certain features — such as date ideas, quiz insights, and trip suggestions — are generated using a third-party AI provider, Anthropic.
  2. When you use one of these features, the inputs needed to produce a response (for example, your prompt and relevant details you provide) are sent to the provider to generate the result.
  3. Your inputs and outputs are not used to train AI models.
  4. AI output is generated automatically, may be inaccurate or incomplete, and is provided for inspiration only. It is not professional, legal, financial, medical, or travel advice.

8. International transfers

We are based in Australia, and the providers described in clause 5 may store and process your information in other countries, including the United States. Where information is transferred across borders, we rely on providers that are expected to apply appropriate safeguards for that transfer. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be processed in these locations.

9. How long we keep your information

  1. We retain your information for as long as your space is active, or as needed to provide the Service to you and your partner.
  2. When you delete your account, we delete your account record from our authentication system and remove you from your shared space. Content you created together may remain available to your partner as part of the shared space, as described in clause 5(b).
  3. Backups and standard server logs are retained for a limited period and then deleted or overwritten in the ordinary course of operations.

10. How we keep your information safe

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect personal information, including access controls that limit a couple space to its two paired members. Because the Service is anonymous-first, we hold very little information capable of identifying you. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children's privacy

The Service is intended for people aged 18 years or older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@next-visit.app and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

  1. You may edit or delete content directly in the app at any time.
  2. You may delete your account from Profile → delete account, which removes your account from our authentication system (so a linked email may be reused) and removes you from your shared space.
  3. You may turn notifications on or off in Profile → Notifications, and you may decline to link an email address if you would prefer that we hold none.
  4. Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, deletion of, or a copy of your personal information, and to object to or request restriction of certain processing. To make a request, contact us using the details in clause 17. We will respond as required by applicable law and may need to verify your identity.

  1. Residents of certain US states may have rights to know, access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of their personal information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information and of targeted advertising.
  2. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under applicable US state privacy laws, and we do not use personal information for targeted advertising.
  3. To exercise any applicable right, contact us using the details in clause 17. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

14. Australia and other regions

We handle personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). If you have a privacy concern, you may contact us using the details in clause 17. 15. Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → delete account within the app. Deletion permanently removes your account from our authentication system and removes you from your shared space. As noted in clause 5(b), content created jointly within a couple space may remain visible to your partner unless they also delete it. If you need help deleting your account or data, contact us at support@next-visit.app.

Your continued use of the Service after a change takes effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
