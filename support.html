Support

We're here to help.

Questions, trouble signing in, or want your data removed? Email support@next-visit.app and a real person will get back to you.

Common questions

I got a new phone — how do I get my account back?

Next Visit can attach an email to your account for exactly this. In the app, open You → account recovery and add your email; we'll send you a secure sign-in link. On your new device, tap that link (or request a recovery link) to sign back into the same space.

How do I connect with my partner?

One of you shares an invite link or a 6-digit pairing code from You → invite your partner. When the other opens the link or enters the code, you're both in the same private space and everything syncs.

Who can see our photos and memories?

Only the two of you. Your space is private — nothing in Next Visit is public, and we don't sell or share your content. See the privacy policy for the full picture.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, open You → delete account. This removes your personal details (name, email, birthday, profile photo, notification tokens) and frees your email for reuse. Shared memories stay available to your partner unless they delete their account too. If you'd like us to handle a deletion for you, email support@next-visit.app.

Is the trip planner the same as the app?

The planner is a separate, no-login tool for sketching out a trip — flights, stays, and things to do. It doesn't create an account or save anything to a server; your trip lives in your browser until you leave.

Still stuck? Email support@next-visit.app — include your partner's name or your space invite link if it's an account issue, and we'll sort it out.
